Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 June 2026

THE DEODORANT MAN ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and why, for our website and the game.

Information we collect

  • Email address — only if you join the waitlist, so we can notify you at launch. You can ask us to delete it any time.
  • Quiz answers — used to generate your Stink Level result; we do not tie them to your identity.
  • Game & usage data — the game uses advertising and analytics SDKs (e.g. AdMob / AppLovin) that may collect device identifiers and app-usage data to serve ads and measure performance. Details are declared in the App Store and Google Play data disclosures.
  • Website analytics — this site may use Google Analytics and advertising cookies to measure traffic and the performance of our ads (e.g. how many visitors join the waitlist). This helps us understand which promotions work. You can block these via your browser or device settings.

How we use it

To send launch and product updates you asked for, to run and improve the game, and to show ads that keep the game free. We do not sell your personal information.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers that help us operate (email delivery, ad mediation, analytics), each under their own privacy terms.

Your choices

Unsubscribe from emails at any time, or email us to access or delete your data. Where required, you can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings.

Children

The game is rated Teen (12+) and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
